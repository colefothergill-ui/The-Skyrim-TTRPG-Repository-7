## Act Structure

### Act I: Battle and Beginnings (5 quests, 4-6 sessions)
Opening with immediate conflict, establishing allegiances

1. **Battle of Whiterun** - Campaign opening, choose faction
2. **Divided Loyalties** - Establish position, guild interactions
3. **The Reach Conspiracy** - Markarth, Forsworn, Silver-Bloods
4. **Shield or Storm** - First major operation for faction
5. **Whispers of Gold** - Thalmor conspiracy discovered (transition to Act II)

**Themes:** War, Allegiance, Manipulation

### Act II: Fractured Skyrim (5 quests, 7-10 sessions)
Investigation and espionage, uncovering Thalmor manipulation

1. **Shadows in the Aftermath** - Track Thalmor sabotage
2. **The Rift Gambit** - Riften, Maven Black-Briar, Thieves Guild
3. **Falkreath's Shadow** - Necromancy, Thalmor dark magic
4. **Diplomatic Immunity** - **MAJOR QUEST** - Embassy infiltration
5. **Blood and Silver** - Use evidence, critical civil war battle

**Themes:** Conspiracy, Infiltration, Religious Persecution

### Act III: Skyrim's Fate (4 quests, 5-7 sessions)
Resolution of civil war and confrontation with Thalmor

1. **Season Unending** - (Conditional) Peace conference with Thalmor evidence
2. **Siege of Windhelm/Solitude** - (Conditional) Decisive military victory
3. **Thalmor Endgame** - Confront Elenwen, defeat Thalmor forces
4. **Epilogue: Season's End** - Resolution and consequences

**Themes:** Decisive Action, Revelation, Endgame

## Branching Paths

### Civil War Faction Choice
- **Imperial Legion**: Order, Empire, pragmatism
- **Stormcloaks**: Freedom, independence, tradition  
- **Neutral/Both**: Difficult path, enables peace route

### Major Decision Points

#### Battle of Whiterun (Act I)
- Support Imperials / Support Stormcloaks / Attempt Neutrality
- Affects: Jarl, hold allegiance, faction relations (-20 to +30)

#### The Reach Conspiracy (Act I)
- Support Silver-Bloods / Aid Forsworn / Expose All
- Affects: Reach stability, Thalmor influence, Forsworn relations

#### Diplomatic Immunity (Act II)
- Share Intel: Imperials / Stormcloaks / Both / Keep Secret
- Affects: Faction trust (+20), peace talks possibility, leverage

#### Act III Resolution
- **Peace Path**: Season Unending conference (+30 both factions)
- **War Path**: Siege of capital (-80 defeated faction)

## Thalmor Conspiracy Arc

### Evidence Gathering
1. **Battle of Whiterun**: Thalmor observers, suspicious behavior
2. **The Reach**: Thalmor arming multiple sides
3. **Shadows in the Aftermath**: Thalmor supply caravans, saboteurs
4. **The Rift**: Information network infiltration
5. **Falkreath**: Necromancy experiments
6. **Diplomatic Immunity**: Complete dossiers and master plan

### Revealed Truths
- Thalmor manipulated Ulfric during captivity
- Goal: Perpetual war to weaken Skyrim
- Ultimate aim: Skyrim annexation
- Necromancy and forbidden magic usage
- Both sides have been played

### Confrontation
- Expose manipulation to faction leaders
- Use evidence in peace negotiations
- Final assault on Thalmor forces
- Defeat Elenwen and expel Thalmor from Skyrim

## GM Guidance

### Campaign Tone
- **Morally Complex**: No pure good/evil factions
- **Politically Intricate**: Multiple layers of manipulation
- **Personally Stakes**: Party choices matter
- **Gritty Realism**: War has costs

### Balancing
- Allow player agency in faction choice
- Both Imperial and Stormcloak paths are valid
- Neutral path is hardest but enables peace
- Thalmor are the true antagonists

### Pacing
- Act I: 4-6 sessions, establishes stakes
- Act II: 7-10 sessions, investigation and revelation
- Act III: 5-7 sessions, resolution and confrontation
- **Total**: 16-23 sessions

### Key Moments
1. **Battle of Whiterun**: Forces faction choice
2. **Diplomatic Immunity**: Major conspiracy reveal
3. **Season Unending/Siege**: Civil war resolution
4. **Thalmor Endgame**: True enemy defeated
